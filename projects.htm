This is a game of Tic-Tac-Toe against an AI(artificial Intelligence). You will never be able to win. At best you will be able to draw! You can choose whether you want to play as 'X' or 'O' and theere is a scoreboard which keeps track of the wins, losses and draws. You can reset the game by pressing the reset button, and this will erase all scores and allow you to choose 'X' or 'O' again. The board will automatically clear after a few seconds...

This is an online version of the Simon game in which you must memorise a series of flashing buttons and repeate the sequence. Each round will be incremented and after certain levels the speed of the sequence increases.You can also use a strict mode in which the game will reset to the first level if you make a mistake, else in normal mode you get as many tries as you like at your current level.
